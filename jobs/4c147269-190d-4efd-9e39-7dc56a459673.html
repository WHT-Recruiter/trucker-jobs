Remote Recruiter – Baton Rouge, LA

This role is built for someone living and breathing the Baton Rouge area who wants to work from home without dealing with local traffic or rigid office hours. We are American Trucking Solutions, and despite our name, this is a straight business development and recruitment position based entirely in a digital office environment. No vehicle involvement, no heavy equipment talk, and zero physical field work.

What You Earn

Your weekly compensation lands between $1,000 and $2,000, paid out under a 1099 arrangement. This is performance-driven but structured to reward consistency and clear communication. You are paid for the results you drive, not for sitting in a chair for eight hours.

Where the Candidates Come From

Every lead you work is already warm. Incoming applications and initial inquiries come to you directly from our marketing funnel. You are not required to cold call, telemarket, or chase down profiles from random lists. Your time is spent screening people who have already raised their hand.

Your Daily Work

You will be on the phone for most of your day, conducting initial calls to assess fit and interest. All tracking and follow-up is done inside our CRM. After a candidate clears your screening, you guide them step-by-step through a remote onboarding process, handling paperwork, verifications, and next steps without ever meeting them in person.

What You Need to Operate

Your toolkit includes a reliable computer, a smartphone that works well, and high-speed internet that can handle video calls and data entry without lag. You must also operate under a formal business structure—an LLC with a federal EIN. If you do not have one yet, do not worry. We include a guided walkthrough during your training to get you set up correctly and legally.

Why Baton Rouge Fits This Work

This city has a strong local work ethic and a community of people who understand the value of a straight conversation. You know the area, and you can communicate with the kind of directness that Louisiana workers respect. Your local insight helps you connect with candidates from Gonzales to Denham Springs, even though everything stays 100% remote.
