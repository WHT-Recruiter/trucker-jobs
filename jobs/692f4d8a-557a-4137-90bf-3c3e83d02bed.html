Our team members consistently take home between $1,000 and $2,000 each week under a straightforward 1099 independent contractor arrangement. Payouts are reliable, and you control your schedule within business hours.

Where Your Candidates Come From

Your pipeline will be filled with warm inbound leads and application inquiries. There is zero cold calling or telemarketing involved. People reach out to us because they want to explore this career path, and your job is to screen them over the phone and guide them forward.

What the Role Actually Looks Like

  • Reporting basic notes on your daily activity—no complex spreadsheets or quotas.

What You Need to Get Started

  • A reliable computer, a smartphone capable of running basic apps, and high-speed internet connection.
  • You must operate as a corporate entity. This means having an LLC with an EIN, or being willing to file for one with our straightforward, guided assistance provided during training.

Why This Setup Works for Longview

If you prefer working from home over commuting through town, this role fits naturally. The schedule offers flexibility around local appointments, and the income structure rewards consistent effort without the pressure of sales tactics.
