Remote Recruitment Specialist (Clarksville, TN)

We are American Trucking Solutions, and our team is searching for a polished and self-starting professional based in the Clarksville area to fill a corporate desk role that focuses on connecting talent with opportunities. This is not a field position; you will work from your home office as an independent contractor.

Your Earnings and Structure

Compensation is straightforward and performance-based. You will earn between $1,000 and $2,000 every week, paid out on a 1099 tax structure. This income is directly tied to your ability to engage with warm prospects and move them through our process.

No Cold Calls, Just Warm Leads

Your day will involve phone-based screening conversations with candidates who have already expressed interest. We supply a steady stream of warm inbound leads and application inquiries. You will never be asked to perform cold calling or telemarketing of any kind.

Your Core Responsibilities

You will be responsible for conducting initial phone interviews, tracking every interaction inside our CRM system, and guiding qualified individuals through a remote onboarding workflow. The focus is on building rapport and ensuring a smooth transition from applicant to active participant.

What You Need to Get Started

To thrive in this role, you must have a reliable computer, a smartphone, and high-speed internet access. Because this is a 1099 independent contractor position, you need to operate as a corporate entity. This means you must have an LLC with an EIN. If you do not currently have one, do not worry. We provide step-by-step guidance during your training period to help you set that up.

This role is specifically open to candidates who live in or near Clarksville, TN, and who are ready to work remotely with a consistent schedule.
