Remote Talent Acquisition Partner – Corporate Desk Support Role

Company: American Trucking Solutions (Corporate Office Support Division)

Your Opportunity in Athens, GA

We are seeking a sharp, self-driven professional to join our remote team as a Talent Acquisition Partner. This is a desk-based, phone-oriented role focused on connecting with candidates who have applied to our open positions. You will be the first point of contact for qualified applicants, guiding them through our streamlined screening and remote onboarding process. This is not a sales position—you are a talent gatekeeper, bringing the right people into our corporate pipeline.

What You Will Earn

Your income is uncapped and performance-based, structured on a 1099 independent contractor agreement. You can expect to earn between $1,000 and $2,000 each week, with payments processed weekly. This is a high-volume, high-reward opportunity with zero cold outreach.

Where Leads Come From

Forget telemarketing or cold calling. All candidate interactions originate from warm inbound leads—people who have already submitted applications or expressed interest through our career platforms. Your job is to qualify, track, and nurture these leads through our CRM system, not to hunt for prospects.

Core Responsibilities

  • Conduct phone-based screening interviews with inbound applicants to assess fit and interest.
  • Accurately log all candidate interactions and status updates in our CRM platform.
  • Guide pre-qualified candidates step-by-step through our remote onboarding paperwork.
  • Maintain a professional, efficient phone presence and manage your own schedule to meet weekly targets.

What You Need to Succeed

  • A reliable computer, smartphone, and high-speed internet connection.
  • Ability to operate as a corporate entity—either an existing LLC with an EIN, or a willingness to file one. We provide step-by-step guidance during training to help you set this up.
  • Strong organizational skills and comfort with basic CRM software.
  • A dedicated workspace free from distractions during scheduled hours.

Why This Role Is Different

This position strips away the pressure of cold calling and replaces it with streamlined talent screening. You will never have to generate your own leads or sell anything. Your focus stays on candidate experience and accurate data entry, making this ideal for someone who loves process and conversation in equal measure.
