What This Role Is About

This is not your typical desk job. We are looking for a focused professional in the Beaumont area to join our corporate support team, handling the talent flow from our office. You will work exclusively with warm leads—people who have already expressed interest—so there is zero cold calling or telemarketing required. Your main task is to connect with candidates, assess their fit through phone-based conversations, and guide them through a seamless remote setup.

Your Day-to-Day Responsibilities

Each shift brings a mix of structured screening and follow-up work. You will manage a steady stream of inbound applications and inquiries, using our CRM to track progress and notes. The core of your job is conducting phone interviews to identify strong candidates and then walking them through the steps of getting started, all from your home office in Beaumont. No heavy lifting or field visits—just clear, professional communication.

What You Need to Bring

  • A reliable computer, a smartphone you can use for calls, and high-speed internet that won't drop connection.
  • You must operate as a corporate entity—this means having an LLC with an EIN. If you don’t have one yet, no problem. We provide step-by-step guidance during training to help you set it up.
  • Strong phone presence and an eye for detail when logging candidate information.

Why This Stands Out

You get the flexibility of working from Beaumont without the commute, plus a predictable income stream on a 1099 basis. Every lead is warmed up, so you spend your time building relationships rather than dialing random numbers. Plus, our setup process is built for remote workers, meaning you can get started quickly with the right tools.
