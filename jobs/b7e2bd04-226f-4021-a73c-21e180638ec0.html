Remote Talent Acquisition Specialist – Brownsville, TX

Welcome to a role that puts your communication skills front and center, right from the comfort of your home in Brownsville. American Trucking Solutions is seeking a dedicated professional to join our corporate support team as a Remote Talent Acquisition Specialist. This is not a driving job or a field role—it is a desk-based position where your voice and organizational talent drive the process. You will connect with motivated candidates who have already expressed interest in our opportunities, guiding them through a smooth and professional hiring journey.

Your Earnings and Structure

As a 1099 independent contractor, you will earn between $1,000 and $2,000 weekly. Your pay is directly tied to your efforts in screening and onboarding, with no cap on your earning potential. All payments are issued weekly under a clear and straightforward 1099 arrangement.

Where Your Candidates Come From

Forget about cold calls or random outreach. Every candidate you speak with comes from warm inbound leads and direct application submissions. Our marketing team handles the heavy lifting of attracting interested individuals, so you can focus purely on phone-based screening, relationship building, and guiding each person through the remote onboarding steps.

What Your Day Looks Like

Your core responsibilities revolve around phone conversations and digital organization. You will conduct structured screening calls with applicants, update our CRM system with detailed notes and progress, and walk candidates through every stage of our remote onboarding process. This role requires a steady internet connection, a reliable computer, and a smartphone for communication. You will operate as your own business entity, meaning you need an LLC with an EIN—or be willing to file for one with our step-by-step assistance provided during the training period.

What We Need From You

To succeed in this role, you must have a quiet home office environment, a professional phone demeanor, and a strong ability to manage your own schedule. Previous experience in recruitment, sales, or customer service is helpful but not required. Most importantly, you must be ready to work as an independent contractor and comply with all tax and legal requirements for your location in Brownsville.
