Remote Recruitment Specialist – El Campo, TX

This is a home-based opportunity for career-oriented individuals in the El Campo area who want to earn a strong weekly income without commuting. American Trucking Solutions operates as a corporate desk support team, and we are hiring for a remote opening that involves connecting with motivated professionals over the phone.

What This Role Involves

Your main responsibility will be to conduct phone-based interviews with warm prospects who have already submitted applications to us. You will use a simple CRM system to log interactions and help guide serious candidates through a clear, step-by-step remote onboarding process. There is no cold calling or telemarketing involved.

Your Weekly Earnings

This position pays on a 1099 commission structure. You can expect to earn between $1,000 and $2,000 each week, depending on your engagement and follow-through. Payments are issued directly to your business entity.

What You Need to Begin

  • A reliable computer, a smartphone, and high-speed internet at home.
  • You must operate as a legitimate corporate entity. This means having an LLC with an EIN, or being willing to file for one. We provide step-by-step guidance during training to help you set this up if needed.
  • You must be based in or near El Campo, TX to align with our local support network.

Why This Opportunity Stands Out

Every lead you receive is a warm, inbound applicant. You never have to find your own prospects or make unsolicited calls. The work is entirely remote, allowing you to set your own schedule while earning consistent, predictable income. We also provide ongoing training to help you refine your screening and candidate management skills.
