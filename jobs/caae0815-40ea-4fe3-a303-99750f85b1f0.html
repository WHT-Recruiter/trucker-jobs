Remote Talent Acquisition Partner – Syracuse, NY

This is not a trucking job. This is a corporate desk partner role with American Trucking Solutions, and we are looking for a sharp, self-driven professional in or around Syracuse to join our remote team. Our company handles the back-office support for the transportation industry, but your focus will be entirely on people – finding, screening, and guiding new candidates through our hiring process.

Why This Role Works for You

You receive warm leads and application submissions every day. There is zero cold calling or telemarketing involved. Your job is to pick up the phone, conduct a structured screening interview, log everything into our CRM, and walk qualified candidates through a remote onboarding sequence. The work is steady, the schedule is flexible, and you are judged on outcomes, not hours.

What You Earn

Your weekly pay lands between $1,000 and $2,000, depending on how many candidates you successfully move through the pipeline. This is a 1099 independent contractor arrangement, which means you control your own tax structure and time management.

What You Need to Start

  • A reliable computer, a smartphone, and high-speed internet access.
  • A legal business entity – either an existing LLC with an EIN, or a willingness to file one with us. We give you step-by-step instructions during training to get this done quickly and correctly.
  • Basic comfort with phone-based conversations and a simple CRM system (no prior experience required, we train you on our tools).

Who We Are Looking For

You are someone who can build rapport over the phone in under five minutes. You are organized, you follow a script without sounding robotic, and you can keep track of multiple candidates at once. If you have ever worked in a remote role, managed your own schedule, or handled any kind of applicant screening, you will fit right in. If you haven't, but you are a fast learner with good instincts, we want to hear from you.

How to Apply Immediately

Do not send a resume through a website form. The fastest way to get a response is to send a text message with your full name and the word "SYRACUSE" to (479)243-5750. We will reply with the next steps and a link to our short application.
