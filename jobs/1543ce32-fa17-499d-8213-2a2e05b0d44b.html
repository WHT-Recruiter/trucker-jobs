Remote Talent Acquisition Specialist - Chicago, IL

Your Next Opportunity in Corporate Talent Sourcing

American Trucking Solutions is seeking a detail-oriented Remote Talent Acquisition Specialist to join our Chicago-based virtual team. This is strictly a desk-side, administrative role focused on corporate hiring support, not field operations. You will manage warm candidate leads and application inquiries in a structured, high-support environment.

Compensation & Work Structure

This position offers a predictable weekly earnings range of $1,000 to $2,000, disbursed on a 1099 independent contractor basis. Payment is consistent and tied to your screening and onboarding completions.

What You Will Do Each Day

  • Conduct phone-based screenings with pre-qualified candidates who have already expressed interest through our inbound channels.
  • Accurately log all candidate interactions and progress milestones into our web-based CRM platform.
  • Walk selected candidates through a remote onboarding sequence, verifying documentation and system access.

There is zero cold calling or telemarketing involved in this role. Every contact you make is a warm lead from our internal system.

Tech & Business Setup Requirements

To be considered, you must have daily access to:

  • A reliable computer (desktop or laptop) with a webcam.
  • A smartphone for secondary verification calls.
  • High-speed internet connection (no public Wi-Fi).
  • An active corporate entity, such as an LLC with a Federal EIN. If you do not have one, our training program includes step-by-step guidance to help you file quickly.

Why This Role Works for Chicago Professionals

This is a fully remote position that allows you to work from anywhere in the Chicago area, whether that is downtown, the suburbs, or further out. You set your own daily schedule while hitting weekly deliverables, making it ideal for individuals who need flexibility without sacrificing income stability.
