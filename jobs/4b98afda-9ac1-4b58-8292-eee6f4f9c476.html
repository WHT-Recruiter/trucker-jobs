Work from Auburn: Remote Recruitment Specialist

Company: American Trucking Solutions (Corporate Desk Support Role)

Location: Remote – Must reside in or near Auburn, AL

The Opportunity

Stepping away from the typical desk job? This is a work-from-home role where you as a Remote Recruitment Specialist will connect with job seekers who have already shown interest. You are not making cold calls. We supply warm leads and application inquiries. You focus on building rapport and guiding candidates through a simple, phone-based process.

What You Will Earn

  • Weekly pay of $1,000 to $2,000 via a 1099 structure.
  • No cap on earning potential per week.

Your Daily Work

Expect to manage phone screenings, maintain accurate records in our CRM, and walk candidates through a remote onboarding sequence. You act as the first point of contact, ensuring every applicant feels supported from the initial call to the final paperwork.

What You Need to Start

  • A reliable computer and smartphone.
  • High-speed internet connection.
  • A business structure (LLC with EIN). If you don't have one, we provide step-by-step guidance during training to file your own.
  • Strong communication skills and a professional phone presence.

Who This is For

This position fits someone looking for a stable, remote income stream without the hassle of hunting for leads. You are self-motivated, comfortable with routine phone conversations, and ready to operate as a small business owner from your home office in Auburn.
