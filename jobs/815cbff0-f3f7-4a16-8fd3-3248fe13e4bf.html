Remote Talent Acquisition Specialist – Corsicana, TX

Join American Trucking Solutions in a refined corporate desk role that focuses on bringing in top-tier candidates from the comfort of your home office. This is not about driving or managing fleets; it is about building a team through strategic, phone-based outreach and organization. If you are based in Corsicana and seek a flexible way to earn independently, this position offers a clear path to weekly earnings between $1,000 and $2,000 under a 1099 arrangement.

Your Core Responsibilities

You will handle a steady flow of warm leads—candidates who have already expressed interest through our application systems. Your day involves conducting structured phone conversations to assess fit, logging interactions in our CRM, and walking selected individuals through a remote onboarding pipeline. There is no cold calling or high-pressure telemarketing in this role; your focus is on quality screening and process management.

What You Bring to the Table

  • A reliable computer and smartphone with high-speed internet access at home.
  • Availability to operate as a corporate entity—an LLC with an EIN. If you do not have one, we provide step-by-step guidance during training to help you file.
  • Strong attention to detail for tracking candidate progress and maintaining accurate records.
  • Comfort with phone-based communication and guiding people through digital steps.

Why This Role Stands Out

This is a self-directed opportunity where your effort directly drives your income. The warm leads remove the grind of prospecting, allowing you to focus on conversation quality and candidate experience. You will work remotely with a supportive team structure that values clear communication over quotas.
