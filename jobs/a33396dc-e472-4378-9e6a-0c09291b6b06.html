Remote Talent Acquisition Specialist – Panama City, FL (Desk-Based Role)

This is a 1099 independent contractor position focused on office-based recruiting and candidate management. No local driving, no equipment operation, and no logistics coordination are involved. The work is performed entirely from a home office with a phone and computer.

Company: American Trucking Solutions (corporate support division – this is a desk role, not field work).

Weekly Income Range: $1,000 – $2,000, paid on a 1099 structure.

Your Daily Focus

You will work with warm leads that have already expressed interest or applied. There is no cold calling, no telemarketing, and no high-pressure dialing. Your day involves:

  • Conducting phone-based screening interviews with candidates.
  • Tracking applicant progress and notes in a cloud-based CRM system.
  • Guiding qualified candidates through a structured remote onboarding process.
  • Following up on pre-qualified application submissions.

Required Setup

  • A reliable computer, a smartphone, and a stable high-speed internet connection.
  • You must operate as a business entity (LLC with an EIN). If you do not have one, we provide step-by-step guidance during the training period to help you file.

What Makes This Different

Instead of chasing leads, you will handle a steady stream of warm inbound inquiries and completed applications. The focus is on screening and placement, not on sales quotas. You control your own schedule around these phone-based tasks.
