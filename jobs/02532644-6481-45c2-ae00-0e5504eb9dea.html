Remote Talent Acquisition Partner – Florence, SC Desk Support Role

This is a local call for professionals in the Florence area who are ready to work from home as a Talent Acquisition Partner with American Trucking Solutions. Please note this is a corporate desk position, not a field or operational role. We are seeking someone who can manage a steady stream of warm leads in a structured, phone-based environment.

Your Weekly Income Potential

You will earn between $1,000 and $2,000 each week, with payments issued on a 1099 contractor basis. Your pay is tied to your ability to guide candidates through a clear screening and onboarding flow, using the tools we provide.

What You Will Work With

Your daily focus will be on warm inbound leads and applicant inquiries. There is no requirement to make outbound cold calls or engage in telemarketing. You will use our CRM to track candidate progress, conduct phone-based interviews, and help each person move step by step through a remote onboarding process.

Your Equipment and Business Setup

To succeed in this role, you will need a reliable computer, a smartphone, and a high-speed internet connection. You must operate as a formal business entity, such as an LLC with an EIN. If you do not yet have one, we will guide you through the simple filing process during your paid training period.

How to Apply for This Florence-Based Remote Role

Do not send a resume or fill out an online form. The only way to be considered is to send a text message with your full name and the word "Partner" to (479)243-5750. Your application will be reviewed promptly, and a local hiring coordinator will reach out to discuss next steps.
