What This Role Is All About

This is a desk-based, office-support position where you work from home to connect with job seekers. You will not drive a truck, operate heavy machinery, or handle logistics. Instead, you will focus on conversation, organization, and guiding people through a clear hiring path. If you live in the Victoria area and want a steady, structured role that blends phone work with digital tools, this might be the perfect fit.

Your Daily Focus

Every day brings a mix of structured tasks. You will start by reviewing warm leads that have already expressed interest—no cold calls or telemarketing. Your main activities will be:

  • Conducting phone-based screening conversations to understand candidate goals and qualifications.
  • Updating our CRM with notes, status changes, and next steps after each call.
  • Walking candidates through the remote onboarding process, from paperwork to system setup.
  • Keeping a clean, organized pipeline so no applicant gets lost or delayed.

What You Need to Succeed

You must have a reliable computer, a smartphone for quick follow-ups, and high-speed internet that supports video calls and cloud-based CRM software. Since this is a 1099 engagement, you will operate as your own corporate entity. If you do not already have an LLC with an EIN, we provide step-by-step guidance during training to help you set this up quickly and correctly.

Why This Is Different

You are not chasing leads. You are responding to people who have already applied or shown interest. This removes the hardest part of recruitment—the prospecting—and lets you focus on building rapport and moving people forward. The weekly pay range of $1,000 to $2,000 depends on how many candidates you successfully guide through the pipeline.
