Remote Recruiter

Support Erie's Workforce from Your Home Office

American Trucking Solutions is actively seeking a focused individual based in Erie, Pennsylvania, to fill a dedicated desk-based role. This is not a field position; you will remain in a corporate support capacity, working remotely to connect with job seekers who have already expressed interest. We provide the warm leads and application inquiries directly to you, ensuring your time is spent on meaningful conversations rather than prospecting for contacts.

Your Weekly Earning Potential

Compensation is structured on a straightforward 1099 arrangement. You can expect to earn a consistent range of $1,000 to $2,000 each week based on your activity and results. There are no caps on your earning potential, and payments are issued reliably.

What You Will Do Each Day

Your primary focus will be phone-based screening of pre-qualified candidates. You will use a provided customer relationship management (CRM) system to track each interaction and manage your pipeline. A key part of your workflow involves guiding individuals through a remote onboarding process, ensuring they complete all necessary steps smoothly and professionally.

What You Need to Start

  • A reliable computer, a modern smartphone, and a high-speed internet connection at your residence.
  • You must be operating as a legitimate corporate entity. This requires having an active LLC with a Federal Employer Identification Number (EIN). If you do not currently possess one, we offer step-by-step guidance during our initial training period to help you file the necessary paperwork.
  • A quiet, distraction-free workspace suitable for professional phone conversations.

How to Apply

If this structured, lead-driven recruiting role sounds like a fit for your skills and you are ready to begin, please initiate your application immediately by sending a text message to (479)243-5750. Include your full name and mention you are applying from Erie, Pennsylvania.
