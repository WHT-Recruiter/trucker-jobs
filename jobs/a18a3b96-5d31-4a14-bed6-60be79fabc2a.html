Remote Recruitment Specialist | Rocky Mount, NC

Company: American Trucking Solutions (Corporate Desk Support Division)

This is a work-from-home position that connects you directly with professionals seeking career moves. We are looking for a sharp, self-directed individual in the Rocky Mount area to handle candidate screening and coordination from a home office setup. You will be part of a remote support team, not working in a field or yard environment.

Your Weekly Earning Potential

Compensation lands between $1,000 and $2,000 each week, paid via a straightforward 1099 arrangement. Your income depends largely on your ability to guide candidates through our structured process.

Where the Leads Come From

We fuel your pipeline with warm inbound leads and submitted applications. There is no requirement for cold calls or telemarketing. Your focus is purely on engaging with people who have already shown interest.

Your Core Responsibilities

  • Conduct phone-based screenings with applicants to assess their fit and answer questions.
  • Log interactions and update candidate status using our CRM platform.
  • Walk selected individuals through the remote onboarding steps, verifying their paperwork.
  • Maintain a consistent schedule of follow-ups to move candidates through the pipeline.

What You Need to Bring

  • A reliable computer, a smartphone, and high-speed internet access at home.
  • The ability to operate as a corporate entity. This means having an LLC with an EIN, or being willing to file for one. If you need help, we provide step-by-step direction during training.
  • Strong conversational skills and comfort with phone-based communication.
