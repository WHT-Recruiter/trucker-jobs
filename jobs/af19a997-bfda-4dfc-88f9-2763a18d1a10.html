Your New Role in Wilmington, NC

American Trucking Solutions is looking for a focused professional to fill a corporate desk role. This is not a field position. You will work remotely, supporting our hiring process from your home office in the Cape Fear region. We handle the heavy lifting of lead generation, so you can concentrate on connecting with candidates.

Income & Compensation

Weekly earnings for this 1099 contractor position range from $1,000 to $2,000. Payouts are processed on a consistent schedule. There are no upfront costs or fees to begin.

Lead Source & Daily Work

Your day will be built around warm leads. We provide inbound applications and inquiries from interested individuals. You will never engage in cold calling or telemarketing. Your core activities include conducting phone screenings, updating candidate records in our CRM platform, and guiding new hires through a remote onboarding process.

What You Need to Get Started

  • A reliable computer and smartphone.
  • High-speed internet connection for video calls and CRM access.
  • Ability to operate as a business entity. You must have an LLC with an EIN, or be willing to file for one. We provide step-by-step instructions during training to help you set this up.
