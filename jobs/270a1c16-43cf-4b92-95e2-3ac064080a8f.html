Remote Talent Acquisition Specialist

Company: American Trucking Solutions (Corporate Desk Support Division)

We are building a specialized, self-motivated team of talent scouts in the West Palm Beach area who prefer to work from home. This is not a field role or a sales position. Instead, you will serve as the first point of contact for qualified individuals who have already expressed interest in joining our network. American Trucking Solutions provides the warm leads; your job is to connect, screen, and guide them toward a seamless remote onboarding experience.

Your Weekly Earning Potential

This role operates on a 1099 independent contractor structure, with weekly payouts ranging from $1,000 to $2,000. Your income is directly tied to your efficiency in handling soft screenings and moving candidates through our CRM pipeline. No cold calling is ever required.

What Your Day Looks Like

  • Work exclusively from your home office in West Palm Beach or its surrounding areas.
  • Handle inbound phone conversations and review new application submissions from our lead system.
  • Use a cloud-based CRM to log interactions, schedule virtual appointments, and track progress.
  • Coach applicants through our step-by-step remote onboarding process, ensuring they understand each stage.

Tools You Must Provide

  • A reliable computer or laptop with a modern operating system.
  • A smartphone for secondary communication and quick follow-ups.
  • High-speed internet connection capable of handling video calls and simultaneous CRM use.
  • You must operate as a legitimate business entity. This means having an LLC with an EIN, or being fully prepared to file for one. We provide clear, guided instructions during your onboarding period to help you complete this step without hassle.

Who We Are Looking For

We need a detail-oriented person who values structure and clear communication. Prior experience in talent acquisition, HR, or phone-based screening is helpful but not mandatory. What matters most is your ability to follow a defined workflow, treat every candidate with respect, and handle a steady flow of warm leads. If you live in or near West Palm Beach and are looking for a consistent remote income stream, this could be the right fit.
