Remote Talent Acquisition Partner - Pensacola, FL

This is a work-from-home opportunity with American Trucking Solutions, a corporate desk and office support operation. We are not a staffing agency, and this role has no connection to driving or fleet management. We are looking for a detail-oriented professional in the Pensacola area who can manage a structured hiring pipeline from their home office.

Your Weekly Earning Potential

Income is straightforward: you will earn between $1,000 and $2,000 each week. All payments are processed on a 1099 independent contractor basis. There are no caps or quotas tied to your earnings, and no weekly minimums required.

Where Your Leads Come From

Your day-to-day will be built around warm inbound leads. Candidates and applicants come to us directly through our online channels and application forms. You will never be asked to cold call, telemarket, or chase down unqualified prospects. Every conversation starts with someone who has already raised their hand.

What You Will Be Doing Each Day

Your primary focus is phone-based screening. You will interview qualified candidates over the phone, accurately capture their details in our CRM system, and then guide each person step-by-step through our remote onboarding process. The work is conversational and process-driven, not high-pressure sales.

Tools and Setup You Need to Succeed

To do this job from your Pensacola home office, you must have a reliable computer (desktop or laptop), a smartphone for secondary communication, and a high-speed internet connection. You must also be prepared to operate as a corporate entity. That means having an LLC with an EIN, or being willing to file for one. Do not worry if you have never done this; our training program includes simple, step-by-step guidance to get your business structure set up correctly.
