Remote Recruiter - Greer, SC Opportunities

About This Role

American Trucking Solutions is actively seeking a Remote Recruiter to handle corporate desk and office support functions from your home base in the Greer area. This is not a driving or field position; you will work within a structured virtual environment, connecting with professionals who have already expressed interest in joining our client base. Your primary focus will be on candidate management and seamless remote coordination.

Earnings Potential

Successful Recruiters in this role typically see weekly earnings between $1,000 and $2,000. Compensation is issued on a 1099 tax structure, giving you control over your invoicing and schedule.

Workflow and Lead Generation

You will be provided with warm inbound leads and completed application forms daily. There is no requirement for cold calling or aggressive telemarketing. Your day involves performing phone-based screenings, updating candidate records within our internal CRM platform, and guiding each new hire through our remote onboarding sequence.

Equipment and Legal Setup

To perform this job, you must have a reliable computer, a smartphone, and high-speed internet access at home. Additionally, you are required to operate as a formal business entity. This means either holding an active LLC with a valid EIN or being prepared to file for one. We provide step-by-step guidance on the filing process during your training period.
