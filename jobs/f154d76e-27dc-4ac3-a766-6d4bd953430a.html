This is not a field or driver-facing position.   • A clear, professional speaking voice and the ability to ask thoughtful, conversational screening questions without reading from a rigid script.
  • Basic comfort with CRM software (we provide a quick walkthrough during your first week).

Why This Setup Works for Sarasota Professionals

Whether you are based near downtown Sarasota, Siesta Key, or out toward Lakewood Ranch, this role is fully remote and built around a flexible schedule. You handle your own hours within a core window each week, and your weekly earnings arrive via your 1099 arrangement. You are essentially running your own small recruitment desk from your home, backed by a steady flow of pre-qualified leads.
